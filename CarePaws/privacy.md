# Privacy Policy — CarePaws

**Last updated:** July 18, 2026

CarePaws ("the app," "we," "our," or "us") is a pet‑care app built to be
private by design. This policy explains, in plain language, what happens to
your information when you use CarePaws.

---

## TL;DR

- **We don't have accounts, servers, or a cloud database.** You never sign in.
- **Your pet data stays on your device.** We (the developer) do not collect,
  receive, store, or sell any of it.
- **No ads. No analytics. No tracking. No selling data.**
- The app makes a **few optional network requests** for specific features
  (finding a nearby vet, showing a map, downloading the on‑device AI, and
  processing a purchase). Those go directly from your device to the relevant
  service — never to us.

---

## 1. Information stored in the app

Everything you enter is stored **only on your device** (in the app's local
storage). This can include:

- Pet profiles — name, species, breed, sex, birthday, weight, microchip ID,
  photos, and notes
- Health data — vaccinations, medications, allergies, conditions, vet visits,
  medical/care records, vitals, and wellness check‑ins
- Calendar events and reminders
- Journal entries, moods, photos, and freeform notes
- Your app settings (theme, notification preferences, premium status)

We have no way to see, access, or retrieve this information. It is not
transmitted to us and is not backed up to any server we control.

## 2. Information we collect

**None.** CarePaws has no user accounts and no backend owned by us. We do not
collect names, emails, device identifiers, contacts, usage analytics, or your
pet data.

## 3. Permissions and why they're used

| Permission | Why it's used | Leaves your device? |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Notifications** | Local reminders for vet visits, meds, and events | No |
| **Location** | Only to find the nearest veterinary clinic on request | See §4 |
| **Photos / Media** | To add pet photos to profiles and journal entries | No — stored locally |
| **Storage / Files** | To export or import your own backup file | No — you control the file |

You can decline any permission; only the related feature will be unavailable.

## 4. Network connections and third parties

CarePaws works largely offline. When you use certain features, your device
connects **directly** to the services below. We do not operate these services,
do not receive the data sent to them, and their use is governed by their own
privacy policies.

- **Find a nearby vet (OpenStreetMap / Overpass API).** When you open the
  "Nearest vet" feature, your device sends your approximate coordinates and a
  search radius to a public OpenStreetMap search service to look up nearby
  veterinary clinics. Your location is used only for that search and is not
  stored by us or tied to your identity.
- **Maps (OpenStreetMap tiles).** Map imagery is loaded from OpenStreetMap
  tile servers, which reveals the map area you are viewing to that provider.
- **On‑device AI model (Hugging Face).** The first time you use AI chat, the
  app downloads a language model file from Hugging Face. This is a standard
  file download; no pet data is sent. After download, the AI runs **entirely
  on your device** — your prompts and pet information never leave your phone.
- **Animated emoji / fonts (Google).** Some animated pet emoji and fonts are
  loaded from Google's public CDN (fonts.gstatic.com).
- **Purchases (Google Play Billing).** If you buy CarePaws Premium, the
  purchase is processed by Google Play. Google handles your payment; we never
  see or store your payment details. See Google's privacy policy for details.

## 5. On‑device AI

CarePaws AI runs locally on your device using a model downloaded once from the
source above. Your questions, your pet's data, and the AI's answers are
processed on‑device and are **not** sent to us or to any AI server.

## 6. Advertising and analytics

CarePaws contains **no advertising SDKs and no analytics or tracking SDKs.**
We do not profile you or track your behavior.

## 7. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. Because
your data never reaches us, there is nothing for us to share. The only data
that leaves your device is what you actively trigger for the features in §4,
sent directly to those services.

## 8. Data retention and deletion

Your data lives on your device for as long as the app is installed. You are in
full control:

- Delete individual pets, records, entries, or events inside the app.
- **Uninstalling the app, or clearing its storage in your device settings,
  permanently removes all CarePaws data from your device.**
- Any backup files you export are yours to keep or delete.

Because we hold none of your data, there is no server‑side copy to request or
delete.

## 9. Children's privacy

CarePaws is a general‑audience app and is not directed to children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data
is collected by us, no personal information about a child is transmitted to us.

## 10. Security

Your information stays within your device's own protected app storage, secured
by your device's operating system and screen lock. As with any app, we
recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a passcode or
biometrics.

## 11. Your rights

Because everything is local and account‑free, you can exercise your rights
directly in the app at any time: view, edit, export, or delete your data. No
request to us is required.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be
reflected here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after
an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
